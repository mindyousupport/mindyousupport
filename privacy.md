What does MindYou do?
MindYou shows a brief, mindful pause before you open the apps you choose to be mindful about, with a personal reminder of what matters to you. You then decide — consciously — whether to continue. It never locks you out.
Why does MindYou need Screen Time access?
MindYou uses Apple's Screen Time (Family Controls) to show your pause screen on the apps you select. That's the only way iOS lets an app do this. MindYou cannot see your activity inside other apps, your messages, or your browsing.
How do I choose or change which apps show a pause?
Open MindYou → the Shield tab → choose your apps. You can add or remove apps anytime.
How do I turn MindYou off?
Remove your selected apps in the Shield tab, or turn off Screen Time access in iOS Settings → Screen Time. You can also delete the app at any time.
Does MindYou collect my data?
No. MindYou has no accounts, no servers, no ads, and no tracking. Everything stays on your device and never leaves it. See our Privacy Policy.
MindYou isn't showing pauses — what's wrong?
1) Make sure you granted Screen Time access (iOS Settings → Screen Time). 2) Make sure you've selected at least one app in the Shield tab. 3) Screen Time features only work on a real device.
