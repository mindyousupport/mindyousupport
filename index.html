Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

The short version: Plausable collects nothing. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no advertising. Everything you enter or that the app observes stays on your device and is never transmitted. Plausable contains no networking code — your information physically cannot be sent anywhere.

What Plausable stores on your device

To do its job, Plausable keeps the following locally on your iPhone only (in the app's private storage and its App Group container, shared solely with Plausable's own Screen Time extensions):

  • The apps you choose to be mindful about. Selected through Apple's Screen Time (Family Controls) picker. Apple provides these as opaque tokens — Plausable cannot see the names, contents, or your activity inside those apps.
  • Your motivation answers. The short responses you give during onboarding (your "why," a person who matters to you, a trigger, a desired outcome). Used only to personalize the reminder messages shown on the pause screen.
  • Optional photos. If you add photos to personalize your pause screen, they are stored locally and shown only to you. They are never uploaded.
  • On-device counts. Simple daily tallies (e.g. how many times a pause appeared) used to vary the reminders. These are numbers on your device, not a profile sent anywhere.

You can remove all of this at any time by changing your selections in the app or deleting the app.

Screen Time / Family Controls

Plausable uses Apple's Family Controls framework with individual authorization — you are setting limits for yourself. Plausable uses this access only to show a mindful pause screen on the apps you chose. It does not, and technically cannot, monitor your browsing, read your messages, or see your activity inside other apps.

Data sharing and selling

None. Plausable does not share, sell, rent, or transmit your information to anyone, because it never leaves your device in the first place.

Children's privacy

Plausable is a personal digital-wellbeing tool and is not directed at children under 13. It collects no personal information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If Plausable's data practices ever change (for example, if an optional cloud feature is added in the future), this policy will be updated and the change described here before that feature ships.
